About Us

From island roots to your next adventure

Who are we

It all began on the winding roads of the Azores, where every turn opens a new view and every journey feels like a discovery. Born from the Bensaude Group, a name with over a century of experience in Azorean tourism, Wayzor carries the spirit of the islands in everything we do.

We are the evolution of Varela Rent-a-Car, a trusted name for both locals and visitors, now reimagined with fresh energy, modern solutions, and a mission to make car rental simple, friendly, and worry-free.

Bensaude Group

Freedom on four wheels

Wayzor specializes in unmanned vehicle rentals, giving you the independence to explore the Azores your way, no queues, no complications, just the open road ahead. Every journey is yours to shape, with the flexibility to stop where and when you choose.

Explore our fleet

A relaxed spirit, with safety at heart

Our style is cheerful and easy-going, but your trust is our priority. Every vehicle is prepared with care, and every step is designed to be clear and hassle-free.

Whether you start your journey in Ponta Delgada, Terceira, or any of our other Wayzor stations across the Azores, you’ll always find the same friendly service and commitment to making your travels smooth and memorable.
